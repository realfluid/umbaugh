Strategic Budgeting for Local Governments: It’s Always Budget Season

Part one in a series - This may sound basic, but have you ever thought, what is a budget? True, it is a list of numbers on prescribed forms utilizing a legislated repository (Gateway). But it’s also a strategic document. A budget identifies actual and projected revenue, defines priorities, maps out a spending plan, and determines your governmental entity’s tax levy and tax rate. It is a tool for management, planning and communications.

Grant Funding Available for Stormwater Projects

Is your community struggling with how to pay for stormwater-related projects? The State of Indiana has grant funding available to help pay for these projects --including developing and implementing watershed management plans and total maximum daily loads among other costs to help reduce nonpoint-source water pollution. In addition grants are available to determine the causes of nonpoint- source pollution problems and develop plans to fix the problems.
